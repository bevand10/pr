Progressive Relaxation

Are the lights dimmed?

Find a comfortable position with your arms resting loosly in your lap, legs uncrossed. Notice your weight sinking into the chair and just let yourself start to unwind. Take a couple of deep breaths, in through your nose and out through your mouth. Let your muscles slowly relax as you gently close your eyes.

If you find your mind wanders during the session, gently bring your attention back to your breathing.
